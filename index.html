About Me

Who I Am

I'm Tyler Le, a software engineering co-op student at the University of Ottawa. I've shipped full-stack apps at federal government agencies (ISED, Global Affairs Canada), won hackathons with React & Supabase projects, and taught kids robotics, all before finishing second year.

I thrive at the intersection of engineering precision and creative problem-solving. Whether I'm automating 15+ end-to-end test scripts or sketching out a user flow, I bring the same mindset: build things that actually work and feel good to use.
